Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the charter price? expand_more

All our charters include the yacht rental, a licensed USCG captain, fuel at cruising speed, and basic refreshments like ice and water. Service fees and gratuity are typically handled at the end of your trip.

Do I need a captain for my rental? expand_more

Yes, for your safety and to ensure a premium experience, all YRSF vessels are operated by licensed, professional captains who know Miami's waters perfectly.

How do I book? expand_more

To ensure the best personal service and availability, all bookings are coordinated through our WhatsApp concierge. Simply click any "Book" button to start a conversation with our team.
